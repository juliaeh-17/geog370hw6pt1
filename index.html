Homework 6, Part 1: Census Data Chloropleth

This is a chloropleth map showing the median family household income in Illinois counties for all races. The highest median family incomes are in DuPage, Kendall, Lake, McHenry, and Will counties, which all surround Cook County, where Chicago is located. The lowest median family incomes occur in the southern tip of the state. The county with the lowest median family household income is Alexander County, with a median of $34,759. The county with the highest family household income is DuPage County, with a median of $96,751. The data does not account for differences between median family household incomes of different races, and does not include non-family households.
